Privacy Policy - Yumel

Last updated: January 14, 2026

Yumel respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use your data.

1. Data Collected

  • Basic information provided by the user (e.g., email address if registration is required)
  • App usage data (movies viewed, lists created)
  • Technical information (device type, OS version)

2. Use of Data

The collected data is only used to:

  • Improve the user experience
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Provide certain features (login, preferences, social rooms)
  • Synchronize your data across devices

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • To comply with the law
  • With service providers necessary for the operation of the Application (Firebase, Apple, Google)

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Yumel does not use cookies on its website. The mobile application may use local storage technologies to improve your user experience.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be deleted immediately upon your request.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, since no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. User Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: You can correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: You can request the deletion of your data
  • Right to portability: You can receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the address below.

8. Contact
